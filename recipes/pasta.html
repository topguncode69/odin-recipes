Description

Pasta is made with a little bit of tomato sauce and cooked maccaronies. Its called Bolognese

Ingredients

  • Maccaronies
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cooking Cream
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook the maccaronies
  2. pour some tomato sauce
  3. Put cooking cream in it
  4. Cook together until its stiff
