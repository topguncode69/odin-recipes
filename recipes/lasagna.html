Lasagna Recipe

Home

Description

Lasagnas are made with white sauce and tomato sauce combined with meat and cheese. You bake them with crust.

Ingredients

  • Crust
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cream
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Set the crust in Cream
  2. pour some tomato sauce
  3. Put cheese on it
  4. Bake at 200 degrees
